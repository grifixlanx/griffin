Here are some of the most famous stories from Roman mythology:

1. The Founding of Rome

The legendary story of Romulus and Remus tells how two twin brothers were raised by a wolf and later founded the city of Rome. Romulus eventually became the first king.

2. The Story of Aeneas

Aeneas was a Trojan hero who traveled to Italy and became an ancestor of the Romans. His journey is told in the epic Aeneid.
